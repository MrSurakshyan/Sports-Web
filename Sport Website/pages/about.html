About Us

The University Basketball Student Organization (UBSO) is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting the sport of basketball within the university community. Founded in 2020, we aim to provide opportunities for students of all skill levels to participate, learn, and enjoy the game.

Mission

Our mission is to foster a strong basketball community, provide resources for skill development, and organize events that engage both players and fans.

History

UBSO started as a small group of basketball enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform for students to play and connect. Over the years, we have grown into a well-established organization with a diverse membership base.

Objectives

  • Promote basketball participation among students.
  • Organize regular practices, games, and tournaments.
  • Provide training resources and coaching for skill development.
  • Engage the university community through events and activities.
